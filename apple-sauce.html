Apple Sause (recipe)

This recipe is based on cooking with a 5 quart pot

  • peel and core apples, cut into pieces and fill 5 quart pot
  • add 1/2 cup water (so they can steam)
  • add 1/3 cup sugar (based on Goodland apples which are sweet)
  • put cover on pot and bring to boil - stirring occasionally
  • cook until mushy
  • mash with potato masher until lumps are removed
  • let cool
  • freeze in containers
